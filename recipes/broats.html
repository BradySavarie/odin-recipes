Broats

A High-Protein Version of Overnight Oats

This overnight oats recipe contains about 60 grams of protein per serving in 610 calories, which is amazing if you're trying to pack on some muscle. Not only is it nutritious, it's also very easy to make and contains only 4 ingredients!

If you're not much of a morning person (I certainly am not), than you most likely struggle with not having enough time to make breakfast before work. I personally can't help but snooze the alarm until the last possible minute. Since this recipe is so quick to make I have found myself consistently eating breakfast again. This has helped me maintain my protein intake so that I don't waste all of my efforts in the gym while also fueling my mind and body in preparation for the rest of the day. No more shakes due to only having coffee before my lunch break.

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder
  • Oats
  • Vanilla Flavoured Greek Yogurt
  • Skim Milk
  • Optional Toppings: Bananas, Blueberries, Almonds

Directions

  1. Mix oats and protein powder together in a mason jar. Shake to combine.
  2. Pour skim milk in and stir until smooth.
  3. Fill remainder of jar with yogurt. Stir again.
  4. Optional: add toppings.
