Bacon Grilled Cheese

A Crunchy, Satisfying Grilled Cheese Sandwich

I've been making this sandwich since I was tall enough to see the stovetop. It's one of the easiest, most satisfying meals on planet Earth.

When you're in a lazy, apathetic mood you can rely on a good ole' grilled cheese to provide adequate sustenance with a minimal amount of effort. Forego the bacon if you can't be bothered (or just buy the pre-cooked stuff).

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Italiano Seasoning

Directions

  1. Heat up a skillet (or cast-iron grill pan for extra oomph) on medium-low.
  2. Spread butter on both sides of each slice of bread.
  3. Slice cheese (~5 pieces per sandwich).
  4. Assemble sandwich.
  5. Cook each side until golden brown. Add the seasoning before the bread gets too crunchy
  6. Devour in under 2 minutes while questioning your life choices. Serve with ketchup.
